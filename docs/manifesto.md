# The PARANOIA Manifesto

We live in an infrastructure owned by others. It's time to build your Ark.

PARANOIA (Pair of Noah) is a manifesto and toolkit for absolute digital sovereignty. We strip your smartphone of autonomy, turning it into a "thin client" to secure your digital life.

## Core Principles

The fundamental problem is that your smartphone is a corporate agent. Apps harvest telemetry, profile you, and sell your attention. We reject this fragmented existence.

In the PARANOIA architecture, your smartphone knows the address of a single server. Nothing more. This is the first step towards true independence.
